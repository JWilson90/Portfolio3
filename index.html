Langford has a strong association with the settlement of the west coast. One of the first settleres to come to the Westshore area is Captain Edward Langford. Langford established a large 600 acre farm for The Husdon Bay Company in what is now known as Colwood.

Since the establishment of Langford, it has grown exponentially in the past few years. This map will show some of the key areas in Langford that have undergone a transformation.
